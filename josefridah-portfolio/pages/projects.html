Case Study 02 — Insurance · Process Automation · Kenyan Alliance

Insurance Process Automation

Eliminating a 10-day underwriting backlog through BA-driven digital transformation

2021 — 2022
The Problem

Manual underwriting and claims workflows had created a 10-day backlog of 200+ cases per week, significantly impacting customer retention. The core system (PURE) had no automated validation, no audit trail, and relied on paper forms and email chains. The business needed transformation — but also a migration to a new EDMS simultaneously.

My Role (Hybrid BA + Engineer)
  • Led as-is/to-be process mapping workshops with underwriters and claims officers
  • Authored SRS documents as single source of truth for dev team
  • Facilitated requirements sign-off workshops with business stakeholders
  • Built & deployed C# automated workflow engine
  • Ran Regression & Integration testing on SQL/PostgreSQL data layers
  • Managed EDMS migration with zero data loss
BA Approach

Conducted structured interviews with underwriters, claims officers, and IT leadership to document current-state flows and pain points. Produced a detailed SRS serving as the single source of truth. Facilitated sign-off workshops that compressed the requirements approval cycle by 25%. Post-build, performed rigorous regression testing on all SQL data layers to validate zero data loss during the parallel EDMS migration.

⚡ TAT: 10 days → 2 days
📊 80% improvement
🗄 Zero data loss in EDMS
😊 Customer retention improved
